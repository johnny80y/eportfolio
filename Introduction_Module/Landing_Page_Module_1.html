First Steps in the MSc Data Science

This first module is teaching us the basics of what it means to become a data scientist. What are the fundamentals of the trade. What is the GDPR, which responsibilities do data scientists bear and what are our future job prospects. Through readings, reflection assignments, and collaborative discussions, we are engaging with key concepts in Data Science.

Update 1: We are encouraged to reflect on our first steps using coding languages such as Python. Given that I have been working with Python for years, I shall not include any reflections on this. I am skipping the lessons on how to define a variable or what a for-loop is. I consider this my first lesson on time management and setting priorities ;)

Update 2: The more we are encouraged to reflect on very basic concepts, the less I feel this portfolio serves any purpose. I have decided to do the required/graded ePortfolio activities and skip the rest. To use my time most efficiently, I focus on coding exercizes and practical skills. Those experiences will be much more beneficial in the long run, as I will probably delete this portfolio after graduation anyway.
